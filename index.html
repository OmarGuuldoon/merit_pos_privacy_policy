Privacy Policy for Merit POS

Last updated: [Date]

Introduction

Merit POS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile Point of Sale application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you configure the app with your Odoo server details
  • Business Data: Product information, customer data, and transaction records stored in your Odoo ERP
  • Session Data: POS session information and temporary data during app usage

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Data: App performance metrics and error logs for debugging
  • Network Information: Connection status and server communication logs

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • App Functionality: To provide POS services and Odoo ERP integration
  • Data Synchronization: To sync data between the app and your Odoo server
  • Performance Optimization: To improve app performance and user experience
  • Error Resolution: To identify and fix technical issues

Data Processing

  • Local Storage: App settings and temporary data stored locally on your device
  • Server Communication: Secure communication with your Odoo ERP server
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your data with third parties

Data Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Local Storage: Sensitive data stored locally with device-level security
  • Access Control: App access controlled by your Odoo user credentials
  • Regular Updates: Security updates provided through app updates

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device and managed by you
  • Server Data: Managed by your Odoo ERP system according to your policies
  • Logs: Temporary logs deleted automatically after 30 days

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: You can access your data through the app or your Odoo ERP
  • Modification: You can modify data through the app or Odoo ERP
  • Deletion: You can delete app data by uninstalling the app
  • Export: You can export data through your Odoo ERP system

App Permissions

  • Camera: Required for barcode scanning functionality
  • Internet: Required for Odoo server communication
  • Network State: Required for connectivity monitoring
  • Wake Lock: Required for continuous scanning operations

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Data Transfers

Your data is processed according to your Odoo ERP server location and your organization's data policies. We do not transfer data internationally beyond your existing Odoo setup.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing notice through app updates
